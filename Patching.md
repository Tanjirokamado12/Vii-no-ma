-Wii No Ma Wad

-Wii C.S Tools

-Dolphin Emulator

-Hxd

Step 1:

Grab Your Wii No Ma WAD (NOT PATCHED)

Step 2:

UnPack Your WAD Using Wii C.S Tools and wadMii.exe (in file your wad and folder same name as your WAD)
